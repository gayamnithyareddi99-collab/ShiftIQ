Our Core Capabilities

One Platform. Every Shift Decision.

ShiftIQ links POS systems, aggregator platforms and staff rotas into one closed-loop operational platform — diagnosing labour and waste inefficiency before it erodes margin, not after.

Scheduling Engine
Module 01

AI Shift Scheduling Engine

Automatically builds optimised rotas by analysing predicted demand, historical sales and live staff availability at shift-level granularity.

Trained on seasonal trading patterns, local events and aggregator order volumes, the engine keeps improving accuracy with every shift it schedules — cutting labour cost inefficiency by an estimated 1.5–3.5 percentage points.
Performance
Module 02

Real-Time Performance Intelligence

Tracks sales-per-hour, upsell rates and task completion live on the shift floor — not in a report after the shift has ended.

Performance data feeds straight back into the scheduling engine, so ShiftIQ learns which staff combinations consistently deliver the strongest shifts and flags underperformance while there's still time to act.
Aggregator AI
Module 03

Unified Aggregator Sales Analytics

Consolidates Deliveroo, Uber Eats and Just Eat data alongside dine-in and takeaway sales into one live operational view.

With delivery channels often representing 40–60% of turnover, this module turns three separate dashboards into a single decision layer for staffing, promotions and pricing.
Waste AI
Module 04

Stock & Waste Optimisation

Flags ingredients trending toward overuse and recommends reorder quantities calibrated to predicted demand, not fixed thresholds.

Turns waste management from an end-of-week report into a continuous, shift-level discipline — protecting margin where food costs often run 28–35% of revenue.
Correlation Engine
Module 05

Shift-Level Correlation Engine

Explains why a shift performed the way it did by linking staffing, waste, delivery demand and revenue outcomes.

The platform's core defensible moat — every shift makes the model smarter, surfacing which team structures and rota patterns actually drive revenue and customer satisfaction.
Compliance
Module 06

Compliance & Checklist Automation

Digitises opening, closing and food-safety checklists with timestamped completion and real-time alerts on missed tasks.

Maintains an auditable compliance trail aligned to UK food safety, allergen and working-time regulations — no more paper checklists lost at the bottom of a drawer.

About ShiftIQ

Turning Fragmented Shift Data Into Predictive Operational Intelligence

ShiftIQ UK Ltd is an AI-powered SaaS company transforming workforce management and operational efficiency in UK hospitality. Our cloud-based platform integrates shift scheduling, labour forecasting, staff performance tracking, sales analytics, food-waste monitoring and third-party delivery data into a single intelligent operational system — helping operators reduce labour costs, cut waste, and make faster, data-driven decisions.

Vision & Mission
Mission

Cutting Cost, Waste & Guesswork

ShiftIQ empowers UK quick-service restaurants, casual dining chains and franchise groups to reduce labour costs, minimise food waste and optimise shift performance through an AI-powered SaaS platform that unifies scheduling, staff performance, sales data, waste monitoring and aggregator analytics into one intelligent dashboard.

Vision

The OS for Modern Food Businesses

To become the operating system for modern food businesses across the UK — empowering every shift manager with AI-driven intelligence previously available only to enterprise chains, and transforming how hospitality operators manage labour, waste and performance in real time.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q01

What exactly does ShiftIQ automate?

+

ShiftIQ automates AI-driven shift scheduling, real-time staff performance tracking, aggregator sales analytics, waste monitoring and compliance checklists — unifying data that's normally spread across 4–6 disconnected tools into one dashboard for shift managers.

Q02

Do I need to replace my existing POS or delivery platforms?

+

No. ShiftIQ is platform-agnostic, sitting above your existing stack — Deliveroo, Uber Eats, Just Eat, Square, Lightspeed, Epos Now — so you keep your current tools while gaining chain-level intelligence.

Q03

How is this different from standard rota software?

+

Most rota tools rely on manual inputs and static rules. ShiftIQ uses demand forecasting and a Shift-Level Correlation Engine that learns from every previous shift's outcomes, continuously improving scheduling accuracy — not just recording who worked when.

Q04

What results can I realistically expect?

+

The platform is designed to reduce labour cost inefficiency by 10–15% and avoidable food waste by 20–30%, with a typical 5-store operator reaching positive ROI within 2–3 months of onboarding.

Q05

Is ShiftIQ compliant with UK employment law?

+

Yes. The platform is built around National Living Wage requirements, age-based wage bands, holiday pay calculations and working time regulations, with automated, audit-ready compliance records.

Q06

How long does onboarding take?

+

Once your first POS and aggregator integration is connected, the configuration template is standardised — reducing onboarding for subsequent sites from weeks to hours.
